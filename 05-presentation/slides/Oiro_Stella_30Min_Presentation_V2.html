Slide 6 of 15
4 Problem Statements
Research-Based Problem Definition

Refined Problem Statements

Problem 1: Mobile Speed

James needs a mobile-first booking system that completes reservations in under 2 minutes because longer processes lead to abandonment and competitor choice

Problem 2: Team Coordination

Sarah needs team booking coordination with advance planning because client meetings require guaranteed professional space and team schedule alignment

Problem 3: Payment Trust

All users need M-Pesa integration with familiar security patterns because 95% prefer mobile money over card payments for local business transactions

Problem 4: Administrative Efficiency

Grace needs automated booking management with real-time oversight because manual processes consume 3+ hours daily and limit business growth

Ideation Insight

These problems led to our three-tier booking concept: Daily (James), Weekly (Sarah), Monthly (David) with unified Admin Dashboard (Grace)
